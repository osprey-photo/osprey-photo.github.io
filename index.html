About Osprey

Simple, yet functional software aimed at camera clubs, who just want it to work.

As long time photographer and member of several clubs, been on comittiees as the Competition Secretary, and having visited many clubs has as a SCPF judge has convinced me that there is a space for effective programs. Being a Software Engineer by day as well has put me in good place to fill this gap.

This project is just making in first venture into the wider-world, it's being tested in my own club. Please get in touch if you want to use any of these applications!
